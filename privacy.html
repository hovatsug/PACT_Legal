Privacy Policy

PACT respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store data when you use our service.

Data We Access

  • Google Calendar events (create, update, delete)
  • Google Tasks (if enabled)
  • Telegram user identifiers

Purpose

Data is used exclusively to create reminders, tasks, and calendar events requested by the user.

Data Storage

Data is stored securely in our database and is never sold or shared with third parties.

Revoking Access

Users can revoke Google access at any time via their Google Account permissions page.

Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their data by contacting support@pact.app.
